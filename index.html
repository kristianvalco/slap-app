Slap your Mac.
It moans.

A tiny menu bar app that reads your MacBook's built-in accelerometer. Slap it — it responds. Harder slap, louder moan.

Download for Mac — Free

macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon & Intel · First launch instructions ↓

📡

Reads the built-in accelerometer

Uses the real hardware sensor — no microphone, no camera. Works on M1 through M4.

💪

Volume scales with force

Gentle tap? Whisper. Full slap? Full volume. Proportional feedback.

🎚️

Adjustable sensitivity

From "earthquake detector" to "needs a running start". Tune it in the menu.

⏱️

Cooldown timer

Prevents meltdowns during rapid slaps. You're welcome, open offices.

🔌

USB Moaner

Plug or unplug a USB device. It moans. We're not sorry.

🔊

Custom sounds

Add your own audio files. Random shuffle or pick one. Your Mac, your rules.

👻

Menu bar only

No dock icon. Lives silently in the menu bar. Always ready.

🏆

Slap counter

Tracks your lifetime slaps in the menu bar. High score pending.

How to install

1
Download Slap.zip and unzip it — you get Slap.app.
2
Drag Slap.app to your /Applications folder.
3
Right-click → Open on the first launch. macOS will warn about an unidentified developer — click Open anyway. (This only happens once.)
4
The 👋 icon appears in your menu bar. Slap your Mac.
